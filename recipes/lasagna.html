Lasagna

Using this recipe you will craft the most delightful lasagna for your friends and family.

This lasagna will probably not be that delicious however as I am making up this recipe.

It may be wise not head any advice from here on out. Attempt at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • 1 box dried pasta sheets
  • 1 Jar pasta sauce
  • 2 8oz containers of marscapone cheese
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 6oz each of shredded parm and mozzerella cheese
  • Seasoning to taste: Salt, Pepper, Italian herbs (Basil, Thyme, etc)
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  2. Fill large pot with water. Place on high heat to boil. Add hearty amount of salt. Water should taste like the ocean.
  3. Place large cast iron skillet on medium high heat. Place liberal amount of olive oil in pan
  4. In large bowl mix ricotta, mozzarella and herbs. Place to the side for later use.
  5. Once pan is hot add ground beef. Let brown on one side before chopping up with spatula.
  6. Once water has come to a boil add pasta sheets and cook according to box instructions.
  7. As meat browns season with salt, pepper and other spices/herbs.
  8. Once meat has been sufficently browned add jar of sauce. Take off heat
  9. Drain cooked pasta sheets and place to side.
  10. Wipe bottom of pyrex baking dish with olive oil.
  11. Begin construction lasagna layers. Place pasta then meat then cheese.
  12. Top with shredded parm and place in oven.
  13. Lasagna will be done when parm has browned and bubbled
  14. Serve to freinds and family
