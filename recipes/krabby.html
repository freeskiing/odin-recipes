Double Krabby Patty with the works

Do you live in a pineapple under the sea?

Are you stuck in a dead end job where your boss makes you pay him for breathing?

If the answer to the following is yes then try this Krabby Patty recipe! Don't forget the pickles!

Ingredients

  • 1 Seaweed bun
  • Sea pickles
  • Maritime Mustard
  • 'Krusty' Ketchup
  • Sea Tomato
  • Sea Onion
  • Sea Lettuce
  • Krabby Patty (Ask Plankton for assistance in acquiring recipe for Krabby Patty secret formula)

Assembly

  1. Slice Seaweed bun in half
  2. Put Patty on bottom bun
  3. Add lettuce, cheese, onoins, tomatoes, ketchup, mustard, pickles and top bun im that order!
  4. Serve to customer

If customer complains that there are no pickles look under customer's tongue. May also find pickles from last time, car keys and ride.
