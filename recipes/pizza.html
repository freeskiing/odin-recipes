Pizza

Have you ever wanted to order a pizza but the phone was too far away?

Use this recipe if you want to be able to make your own pizza without all that pesky dialing or online ordering

Feel free to alter this recipe as you please. If you don't like mozzerella cheese why not try cottage!

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (this can be found at most modern supermarkets
  • 1 55 gallon drum of pizza sauce
  • A metric ton of shredded cheese. Any type will do e.g. mozzerella, cheddar, blue, toe
  • Toppings of your choice. Like fish? Use anchovies!
  • 10 cans pineapple. Use varying types for all that pineapple on pizza goodness
  • Seasoning to taste: Salt, Pepper, Italian herbs, zinc, arsenic, chlorine
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven. Get as hot as possible. If you oven can not reach refractory tempertures add more blowtorches
  2. Use diesel powered road building equipment to flatten ball of dough.
  3. Place flattened dough onto pizza trat and spread liberally with sauce. Very liberally. Use the whole drum.
  4. From airplane cruising altitude sprinkle cheese to ensure adequate coverage of pizza and neighbourhood
  5. Apply toppings. Spread evenly or use to build a tower to the heavens.
  6. Add Pineapple. There can never be enough pineapple. If you think you have used too much pineapple you are wrong.
  7. Place pizza into the self sustaining nuclear fusion reaction that is now your oven.
  8. Pizza will be done cooking in .00000000001 seconds. Do not over cook pizza!
  9. Use food grade chainsaw to cut pizza into rhombus shaped slices.
  10. Serve to freinds and family
